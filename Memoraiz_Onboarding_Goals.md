# Common Onboarding Goals for Memoraiz

When defining your company's goals for Memoraiz, consider the following common objectives that our platform helps achieve:

## 1. Centralize Company Knowledge
- **Goal:** Aggregate scattered documentation (PDFs, Notion, Google Drive) into a single, queryable source of truth.
- **Why:** To reduce time spent searching for information and ensure everyone accesses the most up-to-date data.

## 2. Automate Customer Support
- **Goal:** Deploy an AI assistant to handle Level 1 support queries 24/7.
- **Why:** To lower response times, reduce support ticket volume, and improve customer satisfaction.

## 3. Streamline Employee Onboarding
- **Goal:** Use Memoraiz to answer new hire questions about company policies, procedures, and culture.
- **Why:** To free up HR time and provide instant answers to repetitive questions for new employees.

## 4. Enhance Internal Productivity
- **Goal:** Enable employees to ask complex questions about technical documentation or internal processes.
- **Why:** To speed up workflows and reduce interruptions to subject matter experts.

## 5. Simplify Access to Procedures
- **Goal:** Turn complex manuals and compliance documents into easy-to-understand conversational answers.
- **Why:** To ensure compliance and make operational guidelines more accessible to field staff.

## 6. Improve Data Accessibility
- **Goal:** Make unstructured data (like meeting notes or historic reports) easily searchable and usable.
- **Why:** To leverage hidden insights and institutional knowledge that was previously hard to retrieve.
